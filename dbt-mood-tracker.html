The DBT Intensity Scale

Not just "how are you feeling."
How much.

0
Not present. The emotion isn't there right now.
1
Barely there. You notice it but it's not affecting you much.
2
Mild. Present but manageable without effort.
3
Moderate. Noticeable, requires some attention to manage.
4
Intense. Affecting your behavior, hard to redirect.
5
Overwhelming. The most intense this emotion gets.

This is the scale your therapist uses. Tracking it daily — not just "remembering" how you felt — is what makes the data useful in session.
